RC Car

Simple Remote Controlled Car

  • Wireless NRF24L01 Communication
  • Arduino GIGA R1
  • 3D Printed

Mechanical & Hardware Design

  • 3D Printed
  • Designed in Fusion 360
  • 4 Individual Motors
  • Linkage Steering

Electronics

  • Controlled by an Arduino GIGA R1
  • Motors are driven by 2 L298N Motor Drivers
  • An NRF24L01 is placed at the back to communicate with the controller
  • A Servo controls the linkage steering
  • The whole car is powered by a 3s Lipo Battery
