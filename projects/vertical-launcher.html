Vertical Ball Launcher

A Physics Project

  • Launches a ball perfectly vertical
  • The launch is triggered by a distance sensor
  • 3D printed apart from the spring

Mechanical & Hardware Design

  • The spring is held in compression by a servo motor
  • The servo motor moves out of the way, causing the spring to launch the ball
  • The base is pulled by a string at a constant speed in order for ball to land back in the funnel

Electronics

  • Controlled by an Arduino UNO
  • A distance sensor triggers the servo to launch the ball
  • All electronics are powered by the Arduino, which is powered by the USB cord used to pull the mechanism
