Omnidirectional Robot

Simple remote controlled robot with mechanum wheels

  • Wireless NRF24L01 Communication
  • Arduino MEGA
  • Mecanum Wheels

Mechanical & Hardware Design

  • 3D printed wheel assemblies
  • 2040 aluminum extrusion main support
  • Herringbone drive gears
  • Mecanum wheels
  • Designed in Fusion 360

Electronics

  • Arduino MEGA
  • L298N motor drivers
  • nRF24L01 for controller communication
  • Powered by a 3s Lipo battery
